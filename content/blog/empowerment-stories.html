From Skills to Success: Stories of Empowerment

Posted on July 15, 2024 by Aanya Sharma

Meet three incredible women who transformed their lives and communities after graduating from our Saksham skilling program. Their journeys are a testament to the power of opportunity.

In the bustling lanes of Dharavi, Mumbai, Lakshmi once struggled to make ends meet. Joining our Saksham program, she discovered a talent for tailoring. Today, she runs a small but thriving boutique.

"This program gave me a voice. Before, I never spoke up at home. Now, I am part of the financial decisions for my family."
– Geeta, Program Graduate

These stories of change are what fuel our mission. It isn't just about teaching a skill; it is about restoring dignity and confidence.

Looking Ahead

We aim to scale Project Saksham to reach 5,000 more women in the coming year. With your support, we can create more success stories like Lakshmi's and Geeta's.
