Compassion in Action: A Day in the Life of Our Animal Rescue Team

Posted on May 10, 2024 by Vikram Singh

The phone rings at 2 AM. A stray dog has been hit by a car. For our Karuna animal rescue team, the day has just begun.

Our animal rescue team is on call 24/7. They are the first responders for the voiceless. Dealing with injured animals requires immense patience, skill, and above all, a heart full of empathy.

"Every life is precious. When you see an animal you rescued, once terrified and in pain, now playing... that's what keeps us going."

It is not easy work, but the wagging tails and purrs of gratitude make every sleepless night worth it.
