# Shreekama Foundation - Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the basic visual identity for the Shreekama Foundation website to ensure consistency and a professional appearance.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is warm, sophisticated, and trustworthy. It combines earthy, energetic oranges with clean, neutral tones.

-   **Primary Brand Color (Vibrant Orange)**: `#E85D04`
    -   Used for primary calls-to-action (buttons), active links, and key highlights.
-   **Secondary Brand Color (Muted Orange)**: `#D95F26`
    -   Used for hover states on primary buttons and secondary accents.
-   **Accent Color (Bright Orange)**: `#F48C06`
    -   Used for less critical buttons and highlights to provide variety.
-   **Background (Warm Off-White)**: `#FFFBF5`
    -   The primary background color for most pages, giving a soft, warm feel.
-   **Text (Dark Gray)**: `#1F2937`
    -   Primary color for all body copy for high readability.
-   **Footer Background (Dark Charcoal)**: `#1F2937` or a similar dark gray like `rgb(31 41 55)`.
    -   Provides a strong, stable base for the site.
-   **White**: `#FFFFFF`
    -   Used for text on dark backgrounds and for card/section backgrounds.

## Typography

We use a combination of a classic serif for headings and a modern sans-serif for body text to create a balance between elegance and clarity.

-   **Headings (`h1`, `h2`, `h3`...)**:
    -   **Font**: 'Playfair Display', serif
    -   **Weight**: 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold)
    -   **Usage**: Page titles, section headers. Evokes a sense of credibility and importance.

-   **Body Text (`p`, `li`, labels...)**:
    -   **Font**: 'Inter', sans-serif
    -   **Weight**: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
    -   **Usage**: Paragraphs, navigation links, button text, and all other content. Chosen for its excellent readability on screens.

## Button Styles

Buttons are designed to be clear, accessible, and inviting.

-   **Primary Button**:
    -   **Background**: `bg-[#E85D04]` (Primary Orange)
    -   **Text**: White, Bold
    -   **Hover State**: `bg-[#D95F26]`, `scale-105` transform
    -   **Shape**: Rounded-full or Rounded-lg

-   **Secondary Button (Outlined)**:
    -   **Background**: Transparent
    -   **Text**: White (on dark background) or a dark color
    -   **Border**: 2px, White (on dark) or a dark color
    -   **Hover State**: Background becomes solid (e.g., white), text changes color accordingly.
    -   **Shape**: Rounded-full

-   **Text Link**:
    -   **Text**: `text-[#E85D04]`
    -   **Decoration**: Underline on hover.
